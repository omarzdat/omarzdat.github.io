Still Aware Christmas Wreaths

Still Aware Christmas Wreath Initiative is live again for the 2025 Christmas period!

If you're new to our annual Christmas wreath campaign, each year we honour all babies chasing butterflies above by creating beautiful, personalised digital wreaths.

This is a completely free initiative that we are proud to offer every year with the help of our wonderful volunteers.

All wreaths will be uploaded to our Facebook page on Tuesday, 16th December, when submissions close at 5 pm Adelaide time. Please make sure all spelling and dates are correct when completing the form, as your precious Bub's name will appear exactly as submitted.

If you know someone who may like to order a wreath, please tag them below or share this post. Orders are open until 16 December 12pm.

Sending you all much love and support during this time and always.
