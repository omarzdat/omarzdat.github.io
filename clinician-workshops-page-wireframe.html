Join our next workshop session and gain valuable tools for stillbirth prevention

Still Aware's Clinical Training offers guidance on how to talk to women about stillbirth prevention, better understand fetal movements, and effectively communicate with expectant parents. We translate evidence-based research into easy-to-understand, measurable tools for implementation in clinical and consumer practice.

Stillbirth Prevention Workshops

Key Topics

  • A baby's movements in utero
  • An expectant mother's sleeping position
  • Intuitive pregnancy
  • Action-based communication

Workshop Outcomes

  • Understand maternity care providers' role in detecting and managing risk factors
  • Recognize stillbirth's impact on the community
  • Learn effective communication with expectant families
  • Understand fetal movement monitoring as surveillance
  • Know how, why, and when to discuss stillbirth
  • Identify warning signs requiring investigation
  • Name at-risk indicators for mother and baby
