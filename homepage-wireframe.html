Stillbirth is the devastating loss of a baby beyond 20 weeks of pregnancy. In Australia, 6 babies are stillborn every day‚Äîaffecting thousands of families annually. Whether you're expecting, supporting someone who is, or simply care about preventing preventable loss, understanding stillbirth and its warning signs can save lives. Education and awareness are the most powerful tools we have to protect babies and support families through safer pregnancies.
