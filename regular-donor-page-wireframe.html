Become a Regular Donor

Regular giving provides the stable foundation that allows Still Aware to plan for the future and focus on our mission of preventing stillbirth. Your ongoing support, no matter the amount, helps us continue our vital work in education, awareness, and supporting families. When you become a regular donor, you join a community of dedicated supporters who are making a lasting difference in pregnancy health across Australia.

Why become a Regular Giver?

  • You can choose an amount that suits you, and choose your payment schedule.
  • Should your circumstances change, you can easily amend or stop your payment at any time.
  • Regular Giving is a more effective way to donate than once-off donations, even if smaller in value. This allows us to focus less on the financial situation of our organization, and more on our core mission of keeping babies safe.
  • There are no contracts or paperwork!
