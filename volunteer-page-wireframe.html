Why Work With Us?

Working with Still Aware isn't just about giving back—it's about growing, connecting, and discovering your capacity to create change.

You'll develop new skills, meet incredible people, and be part of something bigger than yourself. Whether you have a few hours a month or can commit to a structured placement, your contribution matters.

Our team members consistently tell us that the experience has enriched their lives in unexpected ways, building confidence, expanding perspectives, and creating lasting friendships.

Choose Your Pathway

We offer two ways to get involved with Still Aware. Choose the option that best fits your situation.

Volunteer

Flexible Involvement

Perfect for those who want to contribute on their own schedule. No fixed commitments—give your time when it works for you.

What to Expect

  • Flexible hours based on your availability
  • Choose from a variety of tasks and projects
  • No minimum time commitment required
  • Support and guidance from our team
  • Be part of a welcoming community

Intern

Structured Placement

Ideal for students seeking Work Integrated Learning (WIL) placements or those wanting a more structured experience with defined learning outcomes.

What to Expect

  • Structured placement with clear objectives
  • Mentorship and professional development
  • Suitable for university WIL requirements
  • Defined project work and responsibilities
  • Reference letter upon completion

WIL Placement Process

For Work Integrated Learning placements, your university needs to initiate contact with Still Aware to establish the placement agreement.

  1. Contact your university's WIL coordinator
  2. Ask them to reach out to Still Aware to establish a placement
  3. Once the university agreement is in place, return here to complete your application

You can still submit this form to register your interest, and we'll follow up once we hear from your university.
